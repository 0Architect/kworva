Privacy Policy & Community Rules

Effective date: July 2026 · Version 1.0

Who we are

Kworva is a peer-to-peer campus marketplace that connects students who need something ("buyers") with students who can provide it ("plugs"). We operate on Nigerian university campuses.

What data we collect

  • Name — the display name you enter during setup. Visible to all users.
  • Campus and area — your school and the part of campus you're usually around. Used to show relevant requests and to match you to nearby plugs. Visible to all users.
  • Posts and offers — the requests you post and the offers you make. Visible to all users while open.
  • Phone number — optional, collected if you choose to add it. Not shown publicly. Only shared with the specific person you connect with in a chat.
  • Device session — an anonymous identifier created automatically when you install the app. This is how you stay logged in without a password. It lives on your device; if you uninstall, it's gone.
  • Usage events — anonymised actions like "request posted" or "message sent" that we use to understand how the app is used. No message content is stored in events.

How we use your data

  • To match your requests to plugs who can help.
  • To show your posts to people nearby who can respond.
  • To notify matched plugs when a new request is posted (push notifications).
  • To improve the product based on aggregate usage patterns.
  • To investigate reports of prohibited content or conduct.

Who can see what

  • All campus users can see: your display name, campus, area, and open requests.
  • Only chat participants can see: messages exchanged in a chat.
  • Only you can see: your phone number (until you share it in a chat).
  • Kworva (us) can see: everything above, plus usage events and reports, for safety and moderation.

Data retention

Your data is held for as long as your account exists. If you uninstall the app and your anonymous session is lost, your profile and posts remain in our database but become inaccessible without account recovery (not available in the current pilot). We will provide a proper deletion mechanism in a future release.

Your rights (NDPA)

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights during the pilot, contact us at the address below.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (EU/US hosted).
  • Expo Push Notifications — delivery of push notifications via Expo's servers.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

Community Rules

Kworva is for legitimate campus help. The following are strictly prohibited and will result in removal from the platform:

  • Exam malpractice — selling leaked questions, answers, or impersonation services for any examination (WAEC, NECO, JAMB, university exams).
  • Fraud and scams — Ponzi schemes, fake investment offers, impersonation, or any deceptive scheme intended to take money from another user.
  • Forged credentials — offering or requesting fake certificates, ID cards, or academic transcripts.
  • Prohibited substances — buying or selling illegal drugs or controlled substances.
  • Sexual services — soliciting or offering sexual services of any kind.
  • Harassment and abuse — threatening, bullying, or harassing other users.
  • Harmful content — any content that is violent, discriminatory, or endangers others.

To report a violation, use the "⋯" menu on any request or chat. We review all reports and take action where warranted.

Contact

Questions or data requests: hello@kworva.app

This policy will be updated as the product evolves. We will notify users of material changes through the app.
